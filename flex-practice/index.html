The Kendama

The ancient toy that is making a comeback...

What is a kendama anyway?

  • Kendama means "ball and cup"
  • Dama means ball
  • Ken means cup

How to Kendama?

Kendama is played by holding the Ken or Dama in one hand while the other piece is hanging by a string. The goal is to toss one side up and cath it either on one of the cups or on the spike. While this may seem daunting at first, with practice and repetition it is a very fun time killer.
